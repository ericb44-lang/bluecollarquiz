Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
The Short Version

We collect your email address when you choose to unlock your full Blue Collar Report Card. We use Google AdSense to display ads. We don't sell your data. We don't spam you. That's most of it — but read the rest if you want the details.

Who We Are

AI Can't Build This (bluecollarquiz.com) is a vocational assessment tool that matches people to blue-collar trades using Holland RIASEC personality theory. The site is operated independently and is not affiliated with any trade union, staffing agency, or educational institution.

For questions about this policy, contact us at:

[email protected]
Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Quiz responses — Your answers to the 15-question assessment are processed locally in your browser to generate your results. We do not store quiz responses unless you choose to unlock your full report card.
  • Email address — When you enter your email to unlock your Blue Collar Report Card, we store that email address along with your top trade match, Holland code, and the job category you selected. Your free-text job description, if you provided one, is also stored.
  • Usage data — Standard web analytics data including page views, browser type, and general geographic region may be collected via Google AdSense and any analytics tools we add in the future.
How We Use Your Information
  • To deliver your Blue Collar Report Card results
  • To send occasional email communications related to the site (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To understand aggregate trends in who takes the quiz and what results they receive — this helps us improve the tool
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address or personal information to any third party
Google AdSense and Cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data when you visit sites that use Google services, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

Data Storage and Security

Email addresses and quiz result data are stored in a secured database. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We retain your email address until you request removal. Quiz result data may be retained in anonymized, aggregated form indefinitely for research and improvement purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your email address and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from any email communications at any time
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to external websites — trade union apprenticeship programs, educational resources, and industry organizations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
